## Compliance Recommendations

If you intend to deploy this software:

### 1. Consult Legal Counsel
Seek advice from professionals familiar with:
- Telecommunications law in your jurisdiction
- Data protection and privacy regulations
- Cryptography and encryption laws
- Export control regulations

### 2. Conduct Legal Risk Assessment
Cover these areas:
- Licensing requirements
- Registration obligations
- Data localization mandates
- Lawful interception compliance

### 3. Implement Additional Controls
As needed for your deployment:
- User agreements and terms of service
- Privacy policies and consent mechanisms
- Data processing agreements
- Incident response procedures

### 4. Russian Federation Users
Specific requirements:
- Register cryptographic tools with FSB if required
- Comply with operator licensing (if applicable)
- Implement Yarovaya Law requirements
- Ensure data localization (personal data of Russian citizens)

### 5. Export Control Compliance
Before distributing or deploying:
- Review applicable export control regulations (U.S. EAR, EU dual-use regulations, Wassenaar Arrangement)
- Determine if export licenses or notifications are required
- Implement technology control plans if distributing internationally
- Document compliance measures

### 6. Data Protection Requirements
For GDPR compliance (EU users):
- Implement data processing agreements
- Conduct Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs) for high-risk processing
- Appoint a Data Protection Officer if required
- Maintain records of processing activities
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures

For CCPA compliance (California users):
- Provide privacy notices
- Honor user rights requests (access, deletion, opt-out)
- Implement reasonable security measures
- Maintain records of consumer requests
